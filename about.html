about

We specialize in the export of premium quality Indian coconuts to major international markets including Malaysia, Singapore, and the UAE.

Established in 2011, SLR Global Trades has evolved into a trusted export house with deep expertise in agricultural exports, particularly coconuts. We work closely with farmers across South India to source semi husked, dehusked, and fully husked coconuts, ensuring the highest quality at competitive pric

Over the years, we have developed a strong supply chain that covers farm gate procurement, hygienic warehousing, customs clearance, and international logistics. This enables us to deliver shipments on time while ensuring full compliance with GCC and ASEAN import standards. Our coconuts are known for their high shelf life, natural freshness, and consistent size, making them ideal for culinary, religious, and cosmetic uses.

As we expand our presence across the Middle East and Southeast Asia, we continue to invest in quality control systems and sustainable sourcing practices, aiming to become one of India’s leading coconut exporters.
